This is the Bureau of Consular Affairs time management application. It will be used to track the usage of time for overseas consular staff among the various categories of services performed. CA needs to track the number of hours spent by consular staff on each major consular activity (NIV, IV, ACS, SOGA, Management, and Training/Outreach) in order to accurately price consular services and ensure that staffing resources are allocated appropriately. Failure to properly account for time spent conducting verious activities may result in errors in staffing orin billing other agencies for CA servises performed.
